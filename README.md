# LazyCookApp

An easy, fast recipe assistant for people who want great meals with minimal effort. LazyCookApp suggests recipes based on what you have, your time, and your skill level — then walks you through cooking them step‑by‑step.

About
-----
LazyCookApp helps users prepare meals quickly with minimal fuss. By providing ingredients you already have, a target cooking time and dietary preferences, the app will return simple recipes and a guided cooking flow.

Features
--------
- Pantry-based recipe suggestions (enter what you have)
- Time-based filtering (e.g., “30 minutes or less”)
- Dietary filters: vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free (configurable)
- Step-by-step guided cooking mode with timers
- Save favorite recipes and shopping lists
- Optional: import recipes from URLs or photos (placeholder)
- Offline-friendly caching for saved recipes (placeholder)

Usage
-----
- Add ingredients to your pantry and press "Find recipes".
- Apply time and dietary filters.
- Tap a recipe to view ingredients and the step-by-step instructions.
- save your favorites to keep recipes available offline and at a quick glance.
